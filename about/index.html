About Momentum AF

Mission

Our Mission: To grow our business with the same honesty and integrity we use to spread optimism and to align with your values. Through marketing and networking, we aim to build a team invested in trust, security, integrity, quality and respect; always promising client satisfaction.

Vision

Our Vision: Illuminating our clients' future through providing exquisite solutions and services by spreading our passions for modernization. Along with our extended services, we enlighten your moments through our extravagant event management, financial assistance, and technical support.

Company Goals

  • Provide high paying jobs for youth in underserved neighborhoods.
  • Create a network for young entrepreneurs to connect with industry veterans.
  • Turn blighted urban communities into thriving business hubs.
  • Establish international pipeline with partners on every continent.
  • Host low cost speaker series every month in major cities across the world.
  • Establish MomentumAF media production company to give us platform to share our knowledge with people who need it.
  • Create MomentumAF venture capital fund.
  • Build schools and centers of learning in developing countries.
